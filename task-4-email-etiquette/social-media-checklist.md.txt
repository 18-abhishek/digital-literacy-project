# 📱 Social Media Best Practices Checklist

## ✅ The Do’s: Building Engagement & Trust

* **Be Authentic:** Share the "behind-the-scenes" and human elements of your brand.
* **Optimize Your Bio:** Ensure your profile picture is high-quality and your bio clearly explains who you are.
* **Engage with Your Audience:** Reply to comments, answer DMs, and acknowledge mentions. 
* **Provide Value:** Follow the **80/20 Rule**: 80% of your content should inform/educate; 20% should be promotional.
* **Use High-Quality Visuals:** Use crisp images and well-edited videos to stand out in the feed.
* **Credit Original Sources:** Always tag or link back to the original creator when sharing curated content.
* **Stay Consistent:** Develop a posting schedule that you can realistically maintain.

---

## ❌ The Don’ts: Avoiding Common Pitfalls

* **Don't "Post and Ghost":** Don't just drop a post and disappear. Engage with others immediately after posting.
* **Don't Overuse Hashtags:** Use 3–5 targeted tags rather than a "cloud" of 30 irrelevant ones.
* **Don't Ignore Negative Feedback:** Address professional criticism publicly to show you value customer service.
* **Don't Buy Followers:** Fake followers ruin your engagement metrics and risk account suspension.
* **Don't Overshare:** Keep a boundary between "transparent" and "unprofessional." 
* **Don't Forget to Proofread:** Typos can undermine your authority and brand trust.
* **Don't Cross-Post Blindly:** Tailor your captions for each specific platform's culture and formatting.
